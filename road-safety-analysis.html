Road Safety Risk Analysis

Identifying risk factors associated with severe road accidents in the UK

Tools: Excel (Pivot Tables, Data Analysis Toolpak,Dashboarding)
Dataset: UK Road Safety Data (307,973 accident records)
Objective: Identify environmental and road-related factors associated with severe accident outcomes to support road safety interventions.

Key Findings:
÷ Rural accidents were more severe than urban accidents.
÷ 60 mph roads recorded the highest severe accident rate.
÷ Darkness without street lighting exhibited the highest severity.
— Roundabouts recorded the lowest severe accident rates.
